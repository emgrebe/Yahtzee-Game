Yahtzee

Welcome to Yahtzee! A single player dice game. The game begins with four dice to roll. You are allowed two more rolls within the round before your turn is up. You can hold the dice by clicking the buttons above each dice and then click on the "Roll Dice" button. Each round can be scored in one of the categories below. One through Six can be scored by adding up the sum of the selected numbers. For a Yahtzee, you must have all four of the same die faces to score the 50pts.
Good luck and "May the odds be ever in your favor."
