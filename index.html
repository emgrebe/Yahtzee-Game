Yahtzee

Welcome to Yahtzee! A single player dice game. The game consists of 12 rounds, with each round beginning with five dice to roll. You are allowed two more rolls within the round before your turn is up. You can hold the dice by clicking the check boxes and then click on the "Roll Dice" button. Each round can be scored in only one of the categories below. One through Six can be scored by adding up the sum of the selected numbers. If your total 'Top Score' (One through Six) is equal or more than 62pts, then you receive a 35pt bonus. A Small Straight consists of 4 consecutive faces and give 40pts. A Large Straight consists of 5 consecutive faces and give 30pts. For a Yahtzee, you must have all five of the same die faces to score the 50pts.
Good luck and "May the odds be ever in your favor."
